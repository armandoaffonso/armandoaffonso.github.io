I started my career as a web designer and front-end developer, but I've decided to study and improve myself in order to work closely with user experience and solve huge problems. I'm a hybrid designer who can work with all the steps of a design process, which means that I am knowledgeable from the research to the front end coding.


I'm currently based in Berlin, Germany, leading the design of GetYourGuide’s Discovery & Search Experience, helping travellers to find experiences they love.


Previously, I worked at OLX Group as a Senior Product Designer,  solving problems through user-centred design and new technologies. OLX Group is a global online marketplace owned by Naspers, one of the largest internet and media group.


Before that, I’ve worked at Verizon Connect, one of the world's largest telematics providers, designing Mobile Applications (iOS, Android), IoT platforms, and tracking technologies for smart vehicles.


In general, I have experience with User Experience, Design Strategy, Design Thinking, Lean & Agile UX and Mobile Applications, conducting interface between business/product and technology areas, ensuring the best practices related to design and product development.
