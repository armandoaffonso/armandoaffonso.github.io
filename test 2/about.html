I'm a Product Designer based in Berlin, Germany. I'm currently the design lead of the GetYourGuide Discovery & Search Experience, helping travellers to find experiences they love. Before that, I've worked at OLX Group and Verizon Connect, solving problems through user-centered and lean design.


Previously, I worked at OLX Group as a Senior Product Designer,  solving problems through user-centred design and new technologies. OLX Group is a global online marketplace owned by Naspers, one of the largest internet and media group.


Before that, I’ve worked at Verizon Connect, one of the world's largest telematics providers, designing Mobile Applications (iOS, Android), IoT and tracking technologies for smart vehicles.

About me

User Experience, Design Strategy, Design Thinking, Lean & Agile UX and Mobile Applications.

I started my career as a web designer and front-end developer, but I've decided to study and improve myself in order to work closely with user experience and solve huge problems. Naturally, I get motivated as stuff gets harder. I feel that my work has meaning when I provide smart solutions to a range of different problems.

 

In general, I have experience with metrics analysis, user research, design thinking and innovation in the insurance, finance, advertising and services areas, conducting interface between business and technology areas, ensuring the best practices related to design and product development.


I have worked for major brands and companies in the Insurance, Banking, Finance and Retail sectors, such as SulAmérica, Allianz, Santander, Banco Sofisa, Pfizer, Lilly, GE, Bridgestone, LG and Ebay. Also, I have worked in retail companies, advertising agencies Digital, startups and consultancies, such as Accenture, VML Brasil, Buscapé Company and Rocket Internet, developed several types of projects.

 

Working with design for more than 12 years, I learned that there is no single methodology for all types of jobs. In order to design anything for another human, we should use different sets of User-Centered Design and Psychology concepts. In addition, other approaches and methodologies complement each other to create a unique and workable experience. That’s why I like to maintain the following things as mindsets: User Centered Design, Design Thinking, Agile and Lean UX.

 

I'm a hybrid designer who can work with all the steps from a design cycle, which means that I am knowledgeable from the research to the front end coding. I believe User Experience is incorporated in all of these disciplines, so I have specialized in all of them in order to deliver the best possible experience and support different business areas.


In addition to these disciplines, I have solid knowledge in:  Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, InDesign, Muse, Animate), Sketch App, Keynote & Google Presentations, Version Control (Git w/ Github and Git w/ Bitbucket), Axure, Omnigraffle, UXPin, Atomic and Balsamiq, Invision, Marvel, Pixate and Pop, HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript (Jquery), Agile Methodology (Scrum), Search Engine Optimization (SEO).
