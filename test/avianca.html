The

Project

Avianca’s project was based on creating a new purchase experience to the user. The aim was to improve the flight ticket search and check-in processes, points and miles, and customer service.

In order to fix the lack of consistency, we used navigation templates that would be used in every website page. I identified every content through a sitemap creation. Then, I created templates to support the different contents, both in desktop and mobile.

 

To maintain consistency, I decided to let the main items in the menu as primary action items, with the overall sitemap with all links above. I also created a submenu in all internal content pages, in order to show the user, in an easy way, the main tasks inside a specific area, such as the Dashboard.

 

In the user dashboard, besides claiming points and miles, it is also possible to see the entire purchase history and next flights.
