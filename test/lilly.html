How to deliver a consistent platform for people searching for information, patients, and doctors? The main challenge was to understand the needs of the medical doctors, how the pharmaceutical industry works, and which approach to use with sensitive content in the digital channels. Since Lilly is a pioneer company in many aspects, we had to understand the optimal approach for every Lilly product, and also the medications category in general.

The

Project

When I was working at Accenture, I had the opportunity of working in this project for Lilly, along with other business and design professionals. We created a new identify for Lilly Brasil’s digital assets and a new streaming platform wherein medical doctors could give online lectures to other medical doctors.

We interviewed the entire Lilly business team and medical directors in Brazil in order to understand the better content approach in digital platforms. We then created a list of mandatory items (due to ethics or law), and a list of insights and ideas.
